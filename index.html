Daniel Bachman

Winter 2014

Daniel Bachman is an American primitive-style guitar player rooted in Fredericksburg, VA. A visionary at his craft at only 22, Bachman has been touring and recording for over a decade now. His latest album, Seven Pines was released on infamous roots label Tompkins Square Records, and landed him a spot at NPR’s Tiny Desk Series, thus placing him alongside some of the true guitar-slinging legends. This year, Bachman has spent over 9 months on the road and will be releasing his next LP on Tompkins Square in October, followed by another release on Bathetic Records this Winter.
